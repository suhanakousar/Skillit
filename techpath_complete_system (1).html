Practice arena — problem structure
Easy (1-2 stars)
Apply what you just learned. Pre-filled template, 3 progressive hints, full solution after hints.
+10 XP
Medium (3-4 stars)
Combine 2-3 concepts. Similar to placement rounds. "Faster than 75% of submissions" feedback.
+50 XP
Hard (5 stars)
Interview-level. Minimal hints. Real FAANG problems. Peer solution discussion after solving.
+150 XP
Weekly contests
3 problems, 90 minutes, college leaderboard, top 10 get special badges. Resets every Monday.
Code judge (Judge0)
Python, C++, Java, JavaScript, Go. Runs in 200ms. Complexity feedback + edge case results.
